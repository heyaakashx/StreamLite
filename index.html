StreamLite

Stream and record your phone's camera for YouTube Shorts.

Host: Receiving & Recording

Live Video Feed (from phone)

Instructions:

  1. Click 'Start Host' to get your **Offer** text.
  2. Copy the **Offer** text and send it to your phone.
  3. On your phone, paste the **Offer**, generate the **Answer**, and paste it back here.
  4. Click 'Connect' to begin the video stream.
  5. To record, click 'Start Recording'.
  6. When you're finished, click 'Stop Recording' and then 'Download Video' to save it.

Client: Sending Video

Local Video (from your camera)

Instructions:

  1. Click 'Start Client' and your browser will ask for camera permission.
  2. Paste the **Offer** text you received from the PC into the box below.
  3. Click 'Generate Answer' to create the response.
  4. Copy the **Answer** and send it back to the PC. The stream will begin automatically.
